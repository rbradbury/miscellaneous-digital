# PRODUCT.md

## What this is

The one-page site for **Miscellaneous Digital** (miscellaneous.digital), the holding company for a collection of small, independently built apps. The site is a public index: what the company is, which apps exist, where to find them.

## Register

brand

## Users

- People who tapped a "made by Miscellaneous Digital" credit inside one of the apps and want to know who's behind it.
- Potential partners, press, or curious developers looking up the company.

## Brand voice

Tidy, wry, utilitarian. The name is the concept: a well-organized drawer of odds and ends. Think office stationery, library card catalogs, rubber stamps — not startup gradients.

Three brand-voice words: **tidy, wry, utilitarian**.

## Apps

1. **Who Made This** — the first app in the collection. (Description to be refined by the owner.)

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages (hero metric, three-card feature grid, gradient text).
- Editorial-magazine affectation (italic display serifs, ruled columns).
- Dark "dev tool" aesthetics; this is a daytime, paper-and-ink brand.

## Strategic principles

- One page, no navigation. The apps index is the content.
- Every app entry is a numbered catalog row: number, name, one-line description, link, status.
- The site should take under a minute to read and feel hand-made, not templated.
