Who Made This

Privacy Policy

Effective July 9, 2026

The short version

Who Made This collects as little as possible. There are no accounts, no ads, and we never sell your data. What you submit to the app is used to answer your question, and that's it.

This policy explains what information Who Made This ("the app") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. The app is made by Miscellaneous Digital ("we", "us"). It applies to the app and to this website.

01What we collect

Content you submit. When you use the app to ask about something (for example, a photo you take or text you enter), that content is sent to our servers so we can generate an answer. It is used for that purpose only.

Diagnostics. We receive basic crash reports and anonymized usage statistics through Apple's standard frameworks, only if you have opted in to sharing analytics with app developers in your device settings. These help us find and fix bugs and contain no personally identifying information.

What we don't collect. The app has no user accounts. We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, precise location, browsing history, or advertising identifiers, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

02How we use it

  • To answer the question you asked in the app.
  • To fix bugs and keep the app working reliably.
  • To understand, in aggregate, which features are used so we can improve them.

We do not use your data for advertising, we do not sell it, and we do not share it with data brokers.

03Service providers

To generate answers, the content you submit may be processed by cloud infrastructure and AI service providers acting on our behalf. These providers are bound by agreements that require them to protect your data at least as strongly as this policy does, and they may not use it for their own purposes.

04Retention and deletion

Content you submit is kept only as long as needed to provide the answer and for short-term operational logging, after which it is deleted. Aggregated statistics that identify no one may be kept longer.

You can request deletion of any data associated with your use of the app by emailing us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days. Because we hold no account data about you, in most cases there is nothing to delete.

05Your choices and rights

  • Camera and photos. The app only accesses your camera or photo library with your permission, which you can revoke at any time in your device's Settings.
  • Analytics. You can disable analytics sharing in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security, Analytics & Improvements.
  • Withdraw consent. You can stop all collection at any time by deleting the app.
  • Access, correction, deletion. Depending on where you live (including the EU/EEA, UK, and California), you may have legal rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Email us and we will honor these requests.

06Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

07Security

Data sent between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit. We limit access to systems that process your data and follow industry-standard practices to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but minimal collection is our first line of defense: what we never collect can never leak.

08Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will post the new version at this address and update the effective date above. Material changes will be called out in the app's release notes.
